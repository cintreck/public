# Consumables
For anything not listed, assume [dangerous](#dangerous) and ask to update the list.

With sufficient time elapsed, a given item can move down a level, becoming more dangerous.

## Safe
Consumption doesn't worsen condition.

- Salt
- Potatoes
- Pasta
- Buckwheat
- Beef
- Cucumbers
- Green Beans
- Olive Oil
- Sunflower Oil

## Moderate
Consumption can worsen condition short-term.

- Lettuce
- Chicken
- Turkey
- Eggs
- Broccoli
- Cauliflower
- Cooked Carrots

## Risky
Consumption can be immediately threatening and worsens condition short-term.

- Rice
- Butter
- Nuts
- Bell Peppers
- Pears
- Apples
- Carrots

## Dangerous
Consumption is immediately threatening and worsens condition long-term.

- Honey
- Cheese
- Soya
- Milk
- Onions
- Garlic
- Vinegar
- Ibuprofen
